## Plan for Events in December

- Christmas D-Day Discount
  - The discount starts at 1,000 KRW on December 1 and increases by 100 KRW eacy day as Christmas approaches.
  - The corresponding amount is deducted from the total order price.
- Weekday Discount (Sunday-Thursday):
  - 2,023 KRW off per dessert item.
- Weekend Discount (Friday, Saturday):
  - 2,023 KRW off per main dish.
- Special Discount
  - On days marked with a star on the event calendar, an additional 1,000 KRW discount is applied to the total order amount.
- Gift Event
  - If the total order amount before discount exceeds 120,000 KRW, 1 bottle of champagne will be given for free.

- All events except the “Christmas D-Day Discount” are valid from December 1 to December 31, 2023.


## Badge assigned according to Benefit Amount

- Different event badges will be awarded based on the total amount of benefits received.
- These badges will be used in the 2024 New Year Event, where each badge will grant a different New Year gift.
  - Over 5,000 KRW: Star Badge
  - Over 10,000 KRW: Tree Badge
  - OVer 20,000 KRW: Santa Badge

## Event Terms & Conditions

1. The event applies only to orders with a total amount of 10,000 KRW or more.
2. Orders consisting of only beverages are not allowed.
3. You can order up to 20 menu items per order. (e.g., Caesar Salad - 1, T-bone Steak - 1, Christmas Pasta - 1, Coke Zero - 3, Ice Cream - 1 → Total: 7 items)
